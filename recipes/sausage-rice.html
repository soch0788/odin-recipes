Sausage and Rice

Description

Apple guoda sausage, rice, and vegetables of your choice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • HEB Apple guoda sausage
  • Vegetables (Brocolli works well)

Steps

  1. Start cooking the rice in a ricemaker
  2. Bring water in a pan to a boil
  3. Add sausage links to the pan (cook for 7 minutes per side)
  4. Cook vegetables (I cook brocolli in a pan for 5 minutes then add water)
  5. Cut sausage up for my kid
  6. Add cut sausage back to pan to sear
  7. serve with rice and vegetables
