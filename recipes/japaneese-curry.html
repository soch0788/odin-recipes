Japaneese Curry

Description:

Japaneese Curry with potatoes and carrots

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Rice
  • Pork
  • Golden Curry cubes

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pan
  2. Start rice cooker cooking rice
  3. Cut up potatoes/carrots to bite size pieces
  4. Add potatoes/carros to pan and cook for 15 minutes
  5. Cook pork on seperate pan
